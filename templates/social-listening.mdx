## Example prompt

```text Social Listening Prompt expandable
Every morning, search for recent posts about [topic] on LinkedIn and Twitter.

Steps:
1. Use web-search to find recent LinkedIn posts mentioning the topic.
2. Use Apify Tweet Scraper to find recent Twitter posts from the past 24 hours mentioning the topic.
3. For each post found, create a 1-sentence summary
4. Send a message to Slack with the results.
```
