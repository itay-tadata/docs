## Overview

Tadata offers pre-built connectors for popular services. Each connector includes authentication, tool definitions, and optimizations - ready to use in minutes.

## Available Connectors

### DevOps & Engineering
- **Linear** - Issue tracking and project management
- **GitHub** - Code repositories and version control
- **Sentry** - Error monitoring and tracking
- **Render** - Cloud hosting and deployment
- **Netlify** - Deploy and host web applications
- **Neon** - Serverless Postgres for developers
- **BuildKit** - CI/CD pipeline and workflow orchestration
- **Jam.dev** - Bug reporting with automatic dev tool snapshots

### Communication & Collaboration
- **Slack** - Team messaging and collaboration
- **Slack Notifier** - Send notifications and alerts to Slack channels

### Data & Backend
- **Supabase** - PostgreSQL database and backend
- **Cloudflare Workers** - Serverless functions at the edge
- **Cloudflare Observability** - Monitoring and observability platform

### Productivity & Documentation
- **Notion** - All-in-one workspace and docs
- **Monday** - Work management and team collaboration
- **Atlassian** - Jira, Confluence, and Compass integration

### CRM & Sales
- **Attio** - Modern CRM platform

### Marketing & Analytics
- **Klaviyo** - AI Email Marketing & SMS

### AI & Search
- **Exa** - AI-powered web search and content retrieval
- **Tavily** - AI-powered search engine for LLMs and RAG
- **AudioScrape** - Audio search with 1M+ hours of content

### Meetings & Voice
- **Fireflies** - AI meeting notes with transcription and search
- **Carbon Voice** - AI voice messaging with transcripts and summaries

### Authentication
- **Clerk** - Authentication and user management
